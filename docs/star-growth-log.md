## Quality signals to track

- Stars from people who also open issues, discussions, or PRs.
- Downloads after each release post.
- Users who complete one full audio practice loop.
- Teachers or groups willing to test with multiple learners.
- Repeat visitors in community discussions.

## Anti-signals

- Stars without any downloads, feedback, or retention.
- Repeated copy-paste posts removed by communities.
- Low-quality comments caused by vague calls to action.
- Users confused about platform availability or privacy.
